Welcome to Student Grade Tracker

This is a Student Grade Tracker where teachers can manage modules, create assignments, add students to modules, and assign grades.

Manage Modules

Teachers can easily manage their modules, add new modules, edit existing ones, and delete outdated ones.

Create Assignments

Create assignments for students within each module. Specify deadlines, instructions, and grading criteria.

Add Students

Add students to specific modules, making it easier to manage student assignments and grades within each class.

Assign Grades

Effortlessly assign grades to student submissions. Keep track of grades and student progress throughout the semester.
